About the App

Transform regular PDFs into booklets effortlessly. Our app rearranges your PDF pages into a professional booklet format ready for printing. With support for both 2-in-1 and 4-in-1 layouts, you can create the perfect booklet for your needs.

Features

2-in-1 Booklet Format: Traditional booklet layout with 2 pages per sheet
4-in-1 Booklet Format: Compact layout with 4 pages per sheet, perfect for small booklets
Simple Interface: Intuitive controls make booklet creation easy
Preview Mode: See how your booklet will look before printing
Optimized for Printing: Automatically arranges pages in the correct order for proper folding

How to Use

Generating a Booklet

Click the "Select PDF File" button
Choose your PDF document from the file picker
Select your preferred layout (2-in-1 or 4-in-1)
Click "Convert to Booklet"
Wait a moment while the process completes
Your booklet-formatted PDF will appear with reordered pages

Tips for Best Results

Use paper that's thick enough to prevent ink bleeding through
For larger documents, consider printing in smaller sections
Test with a few pages first to ensure your printer handles the layout correctly
For professional finish, consider using a stapler designed for booklets
